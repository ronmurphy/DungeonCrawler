Design 4: Compass Rose Navigation

Perfect for RPG navigation and could represent different "directions" in your app - North for Character Info, East for Combat, South for Social/Chat, etc. Each direction could be color-coded and represent logical groupings of your features.

Design 5: Constellation Map

A mystical star map that opens to reveal constellation clusters. Each star cluster represents a different app section. The twinkling stars and celestial theme fit perfectly with fantasy RPGs. The map layout could mirror your hexagon grid concept with stars positioned like hex tiles.

Design 6: Mechanical Gear System

A steampunk-inspired gear mechanism where clicking the main gear causes smaller gears to emerge and rotate. Each gear represents a different system in your app. The mechanical theme suggests interconnected systems and could visually represent how different parts of your character sheet work together.
